# User Guide: DocuChat AI

Welcome to **DocuChat AI**! This guide will help you understand how to use the platform to interact with your PDF documents effectively.

## 1. Introduction
DocuChat AI is an intelligent assistant that "reads" your PDF documents and answers questions based on their content. Unlike general AI, DocuChat AI only uses the information you provide, ensuring the answers are accurate and verifiable.

## 2. Getting Started

### Step 1: Uploading Documents
To begin, you need to provide the AI with context. 
- You can upload multiple PDF files at once.
- **Note:** Only text-based PDFs are supported. Scanned images or photos of documents (OCR) are not currently supported.

### Step 2: Asking Questions
Once your documents are processed, you can ask questions in plain English.
- **Example:** "What are the key risks mentioned in the contract?"
- **Example:** "Summarize the financial results from page 10."

### Step 3: Reading the Results
The AI will provide an answer followed by **Citations**. 
- Citations look like this: `[Document Name - Page X]`.
- Always check the citations to verify the information in the original document.

## 3. Best Practices for Queries
- **Be Specific:** Instead of "Tell me about the document," try "What is the termination period for this agreement?"
- **Contextual Follow-ups:** You can ask follow-up questions like "Can you elaborate on that first point?" as the system remembers your current session.

## 4. Troubleshooting
- **"Answer not found":** This means the information is not present in the PDFs you uploaded. The AI is programmed not to guess.
- **Upload Errors:** Ensure your files are under 20MB and are valid PDF formats.
- **Missing Citations:** If the AI cannot find a specific page, it may list the source as "Unknown". This usually happens if the PDF metadata is malformed.

## 5. Privacy & Data
Your documents are isolated to your specific session. Other users cannot see your files or your chat history. Data is stored temporarily and may be cleared after 24 hours of inactivity.
